It's All About

Teacher

A day in the life of a teacher is filled with activities, educating, laughs, and maybe a tantrum or two. No matter if she's a new teacher or a professional one — she's TALENTED! Teachers from around the world are coming together to compete for a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity while supporting an amazing cause.

Supporting

Children

Talented Teacher is proud to support Children's Miracle Network Hospitals, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that aims to save and improve the lives of as many children as possible. They have significantly helped 170 hospitals in their network provide the best care for children by raising more than $7 billion dollars to date.
