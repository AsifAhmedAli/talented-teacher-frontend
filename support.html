Frquently Asked Questions

The competition is open to all teachers in the USA, from PreK to 12th grade, regardless of the educational institution they teach in.

Simply register on the talentedteacher.org website and create your profile. There is no need for a nomination; teachers can enter the competition directly.

No, only one profile/submission is allowed per teacher. Any additional entries will be disqualified to ensure fairness and equal opportunities for all participants.

Teachers will be evaluated through a public voting system. The number of votes received, either through regular votes or donations, will determine their success in the competition.

No, paying for votes is strictly prohibited. We aim for a fair and transparent competition, and any competitor caught engaging in paid votes may face disqualification.

The winning teacher will receive a prize package worth $15,000, including a $5,000 cash prize, a year's worth of groceries valued at $5,000, and a $5,000 classroom stipend.

While there won't be specific runner-up awards, all participating teachers who receive donations (CARE VOTES) will be eligible to receive a portion of a grant from CGCF Cares, providing support and recognition for their efforts.

Visit the talentedteacher.org website regularly for updates and progress reports. Additionally, follow our social media pages on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok for the most current news, announcements, and highlights.

Absolutely! We encourage teachers and website visitors to share their thoughts, experiences, and excitement about the competition on our social media pages. Engage with us using the official competition hashtags to join the conversation.

The primary partnerships and collaborations for the Talented Teacher competition are with Partnered in Purpose and CGCF Cares. However, we welcome partnerships that share our vision and mission. Please contact us for further information.

For any inquiries or support related to the competition, please visit the Contact Us page on the talentedteacher.org website and fill out the provided form. We will respond to your queries as soon as possible.
